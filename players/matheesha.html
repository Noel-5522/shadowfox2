Matheesha Pathirana

Role: Fast Bowler

Joined CSK: 2022

Nationality: Sri Lanka

Born: 18 December 2002 (age 22 years), Kandy, Sri Lanka

Matheesha Pathirana, often referred to as the "next Malinga" due to his slingy bowling action, is one of the most exciting young fast bowlers in world cricket. Making waves with his pace and toe-crushing yorkers, Pathirana debuted in the IPL for CSK in 2022 and quickly became a fan favorite. He brings raw pace and unpredictability to the death overs, offering a major boost to CSK's bowling strength. Matheesha continues to grow as a cricketer with every match and is considered one of the future stars of the franchise.
