Ravi C. Ashwin

Role: Spin Bowler

Joined CSK: 2009 (Rejoined 2025)

Nationality: Indian

Born: 17 September 1986 (age 38 years), Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Ravichandran Ashwin is one of India’s greatest spin bowlers and a cricketing strategist. Known for his variations, intelligence, and match awareness, Ashwin is a proven performer across all formats. He began his IPL career with CSK and returned to the team in 2025, bringing with him vast experience and tactical mastery. With over 700 international wickets and numerous match-winning performances, Ashwin is a leader on and off the field. His return to CSK adds not only skill but deep cricketing insight to the squad’s bowling department.
