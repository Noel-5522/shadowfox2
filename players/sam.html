Sam Curran

Role: All-Rounder

Joined CSK: 2023

Nationality: English

Born: 3 June 1998 (age 27 years), Northampton, England

Sam Curran is a dynamic all-rounder known for his aggressive batting and effective swing bowling. A vital asset in both the powerplay and death overs, Curran brings versatility to the CSK squad. After making an impression with CSK earlier, he rejoined the team in 2023 with more international experience and maturity. His match-winning abilities, especially under pressure, make him a key player in CSK's lineup. Sam is also known for his fierce competitiveness and sharp cricketing acumen, often turning games around with both bat and ball.
