MS Dhoni

Role: Wicket Keeper

Joined CSK: 2008

Nationality: India

Born: 7 July 1981 (age 43 years), Ranchi, Jharkhand, India

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, affectionately known as "Captain Cool," is a legendary figure in Indian and international cricket. He captained Chennai Super Kings to multiple IPL and Champions League titles and is widely regarded as one of the best finishers in the game. Known for his calm demeanor and sharp cricketing brain, Dhoni led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 Cricket World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. Though he stepped down as CSK captain in 2022, he continues to be the soul of the franchise and an inspiration for millions.
