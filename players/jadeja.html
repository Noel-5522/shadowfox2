Ravindra Jadeja

Role: All-Rounder

Joined CSK: 2012

Nationality: India

Born: 6 December 1988 (age 36 years), Navagam Ghed, Gujarat, India

Ravindra Jadeja is one of the most dynamic all-rounders in modern cricket. Known for his lightning-fast fielding, aggressive batting, and crafty left-arm spin, Jadeja has been an invaluable asset to the Chennai Super Kings since 2012. He has consistently delivered with both bat and ball in pressure situations and has often turned games in CSK’s favor with his all-round performances. Jadeja has represented India across all formats and is known for his never-say-die spirit and game-changing abilities.
