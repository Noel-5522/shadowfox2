Noor Ahmad

Role: Spin Bowler

Joined CSK: 2025

Nationality: Afghanistan

Born: 3 January 2005 (age 20 years), Herat, Afghanistan

Noor Ahmad is a young and immensely talented left-arm wrist spinner from Afghanistan. Known for his deceptive variations and cool temperament, Noor made his presence felt in global T20 leagues before joining CSK in 2025. His tight lines and fearless approach against big hitters make him an asset in the middle overs. Noor represents the new wave of Afghan spin talent and adds depth and variation to the CSK bowling unit, supporting the team in building a strong spin attack alongside veterans.
