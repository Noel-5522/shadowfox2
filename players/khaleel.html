Khaleel Ahmed

Role: Fast Bowler

Joined CSK: 2025

Nationality: India

Born: 5 December 1997 (age 27 years), Tonk, Rajasthan, India

Khaleel Ahmed is a left-arm fast bowler known for his ability to swing the ball at good pace. A product of India’s U-19 system, he broke into the Indian senior team in 2018 and has since been a consistent performer in domestic and IPL cricket. With his height and left-arm angle, Khaleel adds variety to the CSK bowling lineup. His aggressive approach and wicket-taking ability make him a promising addition to the squad for the 2025 season.
