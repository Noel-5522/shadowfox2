Ruturaj Gaikwad

Role: Captain & Opening Batsman

Joined CSK: 2019

Nationality: Indian

Born: 31 January 1997 (age 28 years), Pune, Maharashtra, India

Ruturaj Gaikwad is a technically sound and elegant batsman who has risen rapidly through the ranks with his consistent performances in the IPL and domestic cricket. A calm and composed cricketer, he took over the captaincy mantle from MS Dhoni in 2024, becoming a symbol of the new generation of CSK leadership. He made his IPL debut for CSK in 2019 and rose to prominence in the 2021 season, where he won the Orange Cap for scoring the most runs. Ruturaj has also represented India in T20Is and ODIs. His strong technique and adaptability make him a vital cog in CSK's batting lineup. In 2024, he led India to a gold medal win in the Asian Games and is known for his humility, focus, and tactical acumen on the field.
