Deepak Hooda

Role: Middle-order Batsman

Joined CSK: 2025

Nationality: India

Born: 19 April 1995 (age 30 years), Rohtak, Haryana, India

Deepak Hooda is a dynamic middle-order batsman known for his explosive batting and handy off-spin. He has been a consistent performer in the domestic circuit and brings valuable balance to the CSK batting lineup. Having previously played for franchises like Sunrisers Hyderabad and Lucknow Super Giants, Hooda’s IPL experience and aggressive approach make him a key asset for CSK in the 2025 season. With a reputation for finishing games and fielding excellence, Hooda adds both depth and energy to the team.
