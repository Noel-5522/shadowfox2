Rahul Tripathi

Role: Middle-order Batsman

Joined CSK: 2025

Nationality: Indian

Born: 2 March 1991 (age 34 years), Ranchi, Jharkhand

Rahul Tripathi is a dynamic and aggressive batsman known for his fearless stroke play and ability to accelerate the scoring rate in the middle overs. Having impressed in multiple IPL franchises, he was signed by CSK in 2025 to bring experience and attacking depth to the batting lineup. Tripathi is also a dependable fielder and contributes with vital cameos that often change the course of the match. His consistency and team-first attitude make him a valuable asset in CSK’s middle order.
