Shivam Dube

Role: All-Rounder

Joined CSK: 2021

Nationality: Indian

Born: 26 June 1993 (age 31 years), Mumbai, India

Shivam Dube is a powerful left-handed batsman and medium-pace bowler, renowned for his big-hitting abilities in the middle order. Since joining CSK in 2021, he has become one of the team's most dependable finishers. With his ability to clear boundaries with ease, especially against spinners, he provides much-needed firepower in the death overs. Dube’s bowling offers a handy option as a sixth bowler. Over the years, he has matured under CSK’s system, delivering consistent performances that have earned him accolades and fan admiration.
